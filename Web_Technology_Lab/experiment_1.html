New Year Recipe!

Choclate Truffle Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake filled with choclate, perfect to ring in the New Year!

Ingredients :

  • Cocoa Powder
  • Heavy Cream
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar Powder

Preparation :

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.
  2. Combine and bake at 180°C for 30-35 mins.
  3. Cool and layer with chocolate syrup.

Easter Recipe!

Hot Cross Buns

Spiced sweet buns with a cross on top, traditionally eaten on Good Friday and Easter.

Ingredients :

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Mixed Dried Fruits

Preparation :

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.
  2. Combine and bake at 180°C for 30-35 mins.
  3. Enjoy your tasty cross buns!

Christmas Recipe!

Plum Cake

Rich plum cake with soaked dry fruits and warm spices, a must have for Chistmas celebrations!

Ingredients :

  • Mixed Dry Fruits (soaked in rum if prefferd
  • Cinnamon and Nutmeg
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs

Preparation :

  1. Soak the dry fruits overnight
  2. prepare the batter with flour, sugar, eggs, and cinnamon
  3. mix the dry fruits and bake at 170 degree celsius for 40 minutes.

Diwali Recipe!

Ladoo (Besan Ladoo)

Sweet balls made of roasted gram flour and ghee – a classic Diwali treat!

Ingredients :

  • Besan (Gram flour)
  • Ghee
  • Powdered sugar
  • Cardamom powder

Preparation :

  1. Roast besan in ghee until golden.
  2. Cool slightly and add sugar and cardamom.
  3. Shape into laddoos while warm.
