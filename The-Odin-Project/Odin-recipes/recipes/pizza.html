Easy Tortilla Pizza

Description

This tortilla pizza is extremely easy to make. It is light enough to be a snack, serves well as an appetizer, or is so good that it can be devoured alone! You can use any sort of topping variation. The one below is the classic way I usually prepare it but feel free to experiment.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 inch) soft flour tortilla
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • ½ green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • ⅓ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pinch dried oregano

    • Steps:

    1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
    2. Place tortilla on a baking sheet. Brush with olive olive; sprinkle garlic powder,salt, and pepper on top.
    3. Bake in the preheated oven untill golden, 3 to 5 minutes
    4. Remove from the oven and spread tomato sauce in top; arrange chicken breast, green bell pepper, and green onions over the tomato sauce. Sprinkle with mozzaraella cheese.
